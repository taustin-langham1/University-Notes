.a State what overfitting means, the key issue that it causes for machine learning algorithms
and describe a method of mitigating overfitting in decision trees.
[3 marks]

**Overfitting occurs when a model learns not only the underlying patterns in the training data but also the noise. This causes poor generalisation to new, unseen data.**

The **key issue** is that the model performs well on training data but poorly on test data, indicating **high variance** and low predictive reliability.

In **decision trees**, one method to mitigate overfitting is to **limit the maximum depth** of the tree. This constrains the model's complexity, reducing the risk of capturing noise and forcing the tree to generalize more broadly.
