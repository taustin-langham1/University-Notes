- what is the difference between a functional and structural mental model?

**functional mental model: how to use the model**

**structural mental model: facts about the system**

- What is the difference between a mental model and a conceptual model of a system>

**mental model: internal representation that we form in our mind of how something works**

**conceptual model: high level description of how a system is organized and how it operates**
