Shall: mandatory
Should: Desirable

A requirement focuses on what a system will do,  **not how or why it will do it**
**a requirement must be testable**
**a requirement must not be difficult to verify, as in be subject to opinion**


**Functional Requirements**

**Non-Functional Requirements**: Specify the quality with which a system delivers its services

**Functional Requirements:** Are requirements for what a system should deliver

an example of functional: *The ATM system **shall** enable users to withdraw cash

a requirement must be testable and easy to verify - what a system shall do - not why or how
